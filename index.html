Provider

Smart assistant for cancer treatment planning.

About: Provider App is an AI-powered medical assistant designed to help doctors streamline cancer treatment planning while ensuring compliance with hospital security protocols and industry standards.

  • Integrates with Redox API to securely retrieve patient data using name, last name, and birthdate.
  • Ensures full compliance with HIPAA and FHIR standards.
  • Automatically verifies patient records against hospital certification guidelines.
  • Uses AI to analyze recent imaging scans & lab results in one unified dashboard.
  • Explores AI-powered treatment recommendations with future pathway outcome predictions.

OncoPilot

Enhances medical learning and decision-making.

About: OncoPilot is an AI-driven app designed to assist medical students in understanding cancer treatment recommendations. It enables users to perform treatment calculations, discuss patient cases through an AI chat, and generate accurate treatment plans, all while ensuring patient data privacy.

  • Accurate medical calculations supporting precise oncology treatment planning.
  • AI-powered platform for the next generation of healthcare professionals.
  • Facilitates discussion of patient cases without exposing personal information.
